ABOUT CYBERSHIELD

A comprehensive cybersecurity awareness platform built for education, training, and threat intelligence.

PLATFORM FEATURES

  • 01Real-time live attack feed with severity classification — Critical, High, and Medium threats tracked continuously.
  • 02Comprehensive threat library with 6 major attack categories, detailed statistics, impact metrics, and lifecycle analysis.
  • 03Six awareness training modules with practical Do/Don't guides, progress tracking, and completion status.
  • 0425-question timed cybersecurity quiz with streak bonuses, instant feedback, and detailed score breakdowns.

PLATFORM ARCHITECTURE

CyberShield is a pure frontend single-page application built with HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript — delivering a lightweight, fast, and dependency-free experience that runs entirely in your browser.

The platform features session-based authentication, intelligent threat search, dynamic live feeds, animated statistics, and a fully interactive quiz engine — all without any external frameworks or backend servers.

Security-first design principles guide every feature, from password strength validation on registration to secure session management throughout your experience.
